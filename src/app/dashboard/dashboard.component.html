Web Developer

Job Description

A creative Web Developer is needed to join our team. The ideal candidate will be creatively driven, have a degree in a web design or related subject and some relevant experience, with the ability to show a portfolio. The web designer will be joining the online team designing and developing Front End websites and web applications, for a diverse range of clients.

Requirements

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJs
  • Awareness of frameworks.
  • Previous experience within an agency or commercial environment would be a great advantage.
  • Understanding of Jscript, frameworks and libraries.
  • Understanding of cross browser checking.
  • Experience or understanding of designing for desktop through to mobile devices.
  • Responsive Design
